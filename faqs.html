360 Degree Picture?

A 360-degree picture is a controllable panoramic image that surrounds the original point from which the shot was taken. These photos simulate being in the shoes of a photographer and looking around in any direction as desired. 360 photos can be used with any smartphone or computer. It can also be seen using VR headsets


What is VR?

Virtual Reality (VR) uses computer technology to stimulate a 3D environment. VR takes the user into this 3D 360 degree environment using VR headsets. As soon as a person wears the VR headset he/she enters into a virtual world of saved memories (360 degree videos), 3D animated locations etc.
