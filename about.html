About Virtuaso 360

We live in an age of rapid technological development where many countries have united because of globalisation. People from anywhere around the globe can contact anyone around the globe with the help of internet and telecommunication facilities. Live video streaming, online education, social media and many such technologies have made it easy for people across the globe to stay in touch with each other and to learn new stuffs from anywhere around the world.

Although these technologies are good, they do not give a real life feel and the absence of one’s physical presence is felt. The newest technology which solves this issue is Virtual Reality and 360 degree videos. These amazing technologies give an experience of actually being present in the place of the act; be it speaking to a person, attending a class or going on some amazing amusement park ride by taking the user into a virtual world. Virtual Reality and 360 degree technology are rapidly developing and have a great future. However, these technologies have not been widely used across various fields because of the lack of a perfect platform for the same.

We, at VIRTUASO, are building this global platform where virtual reality and 360 degree technology can be used by anyone across the globe for online education, live video streaming, video conferences, tourism, gaming and much more. VIRTUASO provides services like Online classes in virtual 360 degree classrooms, recording and live streaming videos of different events in 360 degree, virtual tickets to concerts and sports matches, Business conference meetings in virtual 360 degree meeting halls and many more.

Our mission is to provide the best platform where our customers can experience amazing virtual reality and 360degree based services of great quality.
